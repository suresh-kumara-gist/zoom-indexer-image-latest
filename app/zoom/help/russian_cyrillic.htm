Russian (Cyrillic)

    

Russian websites commonly use one of three encodings: KOI8, CP1251 (aka "windows-1251"), and Unicode UTF-8.

If your website uses KOI8 or “windows-1251” encoding:

•Select “Specify other encoding” in the Configuration window, and the name of the encoding you are using (ie. “windows-1251”), before indexing your website.

If your website uses UTF-8 encoding:

•Select “Use Unicode (UTF-8 encoding)” in the Configuration window before indexing your website.
